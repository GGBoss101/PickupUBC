About Us

Who We Are

UBC Pickup is a passionate community of environmental advocates dedicated to cleaning up our world. Founded in 2024, we've grown to host over 200 past attendees working together to combat litter and promote environmental sustainability.

Our Mission

To create a cleaner, healthier environment by organizing regular trash collection drives and raising awareness about waste management and environmental responsibility in our community.

What We Do

We organize weekly cleanup events at UBC. We work to remove litter and recyclables, sorting waste responsibly. We also carry out clean ups for other community organizations.

When & Where

Join us for our regular cleanups: every Week on Sundays 11:00am to 12:00pm. Meet at The Nest (Near MacInnes Field).

Join Us

Whether you're an environmental enthusiast or just want to give back to your community, there's a place for you in UBC Pickup. All skill levels and ages are welcome. Contact us to learn about upcoming events and how to get involved! Join our mailing list linked below!
